How to Achieve a Classic Red Lip

A classic red lip is timeless, elegant, and can instantly elevate any look. Follow these steps to perfect your pout:

  1. Prep Your Lips

    Start with smooth lips. Gently exfoliate using a lip scrub or a soft toothbrush to remove any dry flakes. Follow up with a hydrating lip balm and let it sink in for a few minutes. Blot off any excess before applying color.

  2. Line Your Lips

    Choose a red lip liner that matches your lipstick shade. Outline your natural lip line precisely. For extra longevity and to prevent feathering, lightly fill in your entire lip with the liner.

  3. Apply Red Lipstick

    Using a lip brush for precision or directly from the bullet, apply your red lipstick. Start from the center of your lips and work outwards. For an opaque finish, apply two thin coats, blotting with a tissue between each application.

  4. Blot and Set

    Gently blot your lips with a tissue to remove any excess product. For extra staying power, place a thin tissue over your lips and lightly dust translucent powder over the tissue. This will help set the color without dulling it.

  5. Clean Up Edges

    Using a small, flat brush and a tiny amount of concealer or foundation, clean up any smudges or uneven edges around your lips. This creates a sharp, defined line and makes your red lip pop.

  6. Optional: Add Gloss

    If you prefer a glossy finish, apply a clear or red-tinted lip gloss just to the center of your lips. This adds dimension without making the entire lip too slippery.

And there you have it! A perfectly applied classic red lip that's sure to turn heads.
