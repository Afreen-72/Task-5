Mastering the Bold Eyeliner Look

A bold eyeliner look can make a powerful statement, adding drama and definition to your eyes. Here's how to create a sharp and striking line:

  1. Prime Your Lids

    Start with an eyeshadow primer on your eyelids. This creates a smooth canvas and helps your eyeliner last longer without smudging or fading.

  2. Choose Your Eyeliner

    For a truly bold look, liquid eyeliner (pen or pot form) or a gel liner applied with a brush works best. These offer intense color payoff and sharp lines.

  3. Create Your Guide (Optional)

    If you're new to bold liner, you can start by drawing a thin line first. Or, use a piece of tape from your lower lash line pointing towards the tail of your eyebrow for a perfect angle for your wing.

  4. Draw the Wing

    From your lower lash line, draw a diagonal line upwards towards the end of your eyebrow. Then, from the tip of that line, draw a line back down towards the middle of your eyelid, creating an open triangle.

  5. Fill and Connect

    Fill in the triangle you've created. Then, draw a line along your upper lash line, connecting it to the filled-in wing. Make this line as thick as you desire for your bold look.

  6. Refine and Sharpen

    Use a small, angled brush with a tiny bit of concealer or foundation to clean up any wobbly lines or smudges. This will give you that incredibly sharp, crisp edge that defines a bold liner look.

  7. Finish with Mascara

    Complete your eye makeup with a generous coat of volumizing mascara to make your lashes pop and complement your dramatic eyeliner.

Practice makes perfect with bold eyeliner, so don't be afraid to experiment!
